General Guidelines

Welcome to IntelliChem Lab! Follow these guidelines to make the most of your experience:

  • Always read the experiment instructions carefully before starting.
  • Ensure you have all the required materials before beginning an experiment.
  • Follow safety protocols at all times.
  • Report any issues or bugs to the support team.

Experiment Guidelines

Here are some tips for conducting experiments effectively:

  1. Set up your workspace in a clean and organized manner.
  2. Wear appropriate safety gear (e.g., gloves, goggles).
  3. Follow the step-by-step instructions provided for each experiment.
  4. Record your observations and results accurately.
  5. Clean up your workspace after completing the experiment.

Quiz Guidelines

Follow these tips to excel in quizzes:

  • Review the experiment and theory before attempting a quiz.
  • Read each question carefully before answering.
  • Use the hints provided if you're unsure about an answer.
  • Check your answers before submitting the quiz.

Safety Guidelines

Safety is our top priority. Follow these rules to stay safe:

  • Never perform experiments without adult supervision (if required).
  • Avoid direct contact with chemicals.
  • Use proper ventilation when working with gases or fumes.
  • In case of an accident, report it immediately and seek help.

IoT Integration Guidelines

Learn how to use IoT devices effectively in your experiments:

  1. Connect the IoT sensors to your device as per the instructions.
  2. Ensure the sensors are calibrated before use.
  3. Monitor real-time data on the dashboard.
  4. Interpret the data to draw conclusions from your experiments.
